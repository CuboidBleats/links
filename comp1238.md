## Week 3 - File Structue
- Character Names worth remebering:
  _ underscore – (ex: file_name)
  ^ caret – (ex: 2^n)
  ~ tilde – (ex: cd ~)
  \ backslash – (ex: \n)
  ` backtick, backquote
  | pipe, vertical line
